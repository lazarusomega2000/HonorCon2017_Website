Do You Have What It Takes to Survive in Larrys World?
Share
Tweet

Raleigh, North Carolina, September 21st, 2017 – Have you ever wanted to play in your favorite author’s mind for just a bit? Wouldn’t it be fun to just see what it would be like to try to survive when they’re in control? Well now you have the opportunity since we’ve talked Larry Corriea into Gamemastering at HonorCon 2017 for charity! Given that his favored genre is a hard mix of high end weaponry coupled with zombies, vampires and other critters that go bump in the night, the experience promises to be pretty challenging.

So come join Larry Correia, author of the Monster Hunter International series, as he GMs a special one night role playing game session for the upcoming Savage Worlds Monster Hunter game. Welcome MHI graduating newbie class of 2017. Evil looms. Cowboy up. Kill it. Get paid. Open for up to six players. Game session should last about four or five hours. You do not need to know Savage Worlds rules and can learn them as you go.

The auction is located HERE or simply click the tab under programming at our website!

All proceeds go to this year’s charity: Saving Grace K9s!

About HonorCon

HonorCon is an annual celebration of military science fiction and gaming, sponsored by the Royal Manticoran Navy: An Honor Harrington fan association. Named after the main character featured in David Weber’s Honor Harrington series, HonorCon focuses on bringing military science fiction themed authors and special guests to their fans. HonorCon is home to the Rampant Manticore Awards as well as multiple gaming events and opportunities to discuss ideas in unique panels with your favorite authors and their fans. This year’s convention will be held in Raleigh, North Carolina, at the Hilton North Raleigh/Midtown, from October 27th to October 29th.

Raleigh, North Carolina, September 5th, 2017 – After some bumps and grinds, our annual rebate code in honor of DragonCon is online and active. Every year we like to give a little back to the community by providing our tickets at a reduced price during DragonCon because we know just how expensive it is for Con-goers to go to their favorite conventions. This year we are giving a ten-dollar rebate for all purchases until 11:45 (Eastern Standard Time) September 6th, 2017. Use the following code when you purchase your ticket in EventBrite to receive your rebate:

Thank you very much! We hope to see you soon and remember, this code is only good until 11:45 (Eastern Standard Time) on September 6th, 2017
