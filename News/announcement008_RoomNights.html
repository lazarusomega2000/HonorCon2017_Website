About HonorCon

HonorCon is an annual celebration of military science fiction and gaming, sponsored by the Royal Manticoran Navy: An Honor Harrington fan association. Named after the main character featured in David Weber’s Honor Harrington series, HonorCon focuses on bringing military science fiction themed authors and special guests to their fans. HonorCon is home to the Rampant Manticore Awards as well as multiple gaming events and opportunities to discuss ideas in unique panels with your favorite authors and their fans. This year’s convention will be held in Raleigh, North Carolina, at the Hilton North Raleigh/Midtown, from October 27th to October 29th.
