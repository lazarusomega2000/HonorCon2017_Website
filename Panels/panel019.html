TRMN Amateur Radio Auxiliary (W4RMN)

Hermes Buoys may not exist yet, but there is still a constant flood of radio communications moving across the planet 24/7. Come and join the TRMN Amateur Radio Auxiliary and learn about ham radio, some advancements that have been made in the past few years in the digital realm, and potentially how you can get your own amateur radio license to talk all over the world instantly!
