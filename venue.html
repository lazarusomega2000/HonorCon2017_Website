Dealer's Room

Every year we have some of the best science fiction and fantasy related dealers join our convention. In the past we've had dealers such as:

  • Event Horizon Games
  • Final Sword Productions
  • Weber Universal Outfitters
  • and many others

If you would like to be a part of our Dealer's Room, please feel free to contact our convention dealer's room coordinator at: dealers@honorcon.org

Gaming Room

You will find every kind of game imaginable at HonorCon. From tactical board and card games to sophisticated starship bridge simulators, come and play something new or old at HonorCon. This year, we have a wide range of gaming scheduled:

  • SITS Training
  • Star Wars Armada Tournament
  • Star Wars X-Wing Tournament
  • Hullbreach
  • Olympus, Inc.
  • Starfinder
  • and many others

If you have a game you would like to showcase or run a tournament for at our event please contact us at: gaming@honorcon.org

Con Suite

Come One, Come All!

Enter and be welcome to the ConSuite. Looking for a quick bit to eat or a place to rest and recharge before enjoying more of the Convention? If so, swing by. Its relaxed and low-key atmosphere is the perfect place to fill your belly or wet your palate. While the menu may be simple, the food is always hot and fresh and the drinks plentiful. With ample seating and the opportunity to catch-up with friends, the ConSuite is the place you definitely want to visit.
